Mission in 2022 FC Barcelona

Mission

Manager Xavi’s biggest mission for 2022 is to Make Barcelona Brave Again. This very season( 2021-22) Barcelona having very tough time since It's started.Losing back to back matches in the UCL knocked them out of the tournament from the group stage for the first time in a span of time two decades.So gaining confidence will be the main mission for Barcelona this year.

Vission

Barcelona's Vission for this year is pretty clear they are wanting to sign some of the big names in the upcoming summer transfer window. Recently they have signed another promising young player Ferran Torres from Manchester City. So their vission is to sign as many good players they can in this year.

Goals

Despite having tough time on and off the field this season Barcelona willing to win atleast a single Trophy in this year.Weather It's Copa Del Rey, UEFA EUROPA LEAGUE, Spanish Super Cup or Even Laliga's League title.So that's what they are thinking at the moment and that's their goal for this year

Progress Without compromising

FC Barcelona can't compromise their progress with anything. They have to work really hard to get back their glory and they certainly got that capability and talents in hand. So their has to be no looking back their approach for Barcelona this year.
